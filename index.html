A little story about me!

The creative adult is the child who survived!

With freshly finished design courses, I started work at a small agency with our biggest client being Dischem. This is where i learned how to put knowledge to the test & what it was like designing on tight deadlines, working late hours & being involved in all aspects of a business.

Later i was poached by a sister-company who was looking for a full time designer to join them in-house. For 6 years I was solely responsible for running their whole marketing, design and web maintenance department, and also took on the role of Technical Manager and running a team of technicians. I also took on responsibility of the Faulty Returns Manager, and the smooth running and organisation of these 3 departments.

I left after 6 years to pursue a career as a Wedding Planner, having finished a course in Advanced Wedding Planning and to reconnect with my creative juices I felt where being neglected after 6 years with one company. I grew a very successfull business and also started a second company focussing mainly on wedding stationery design.
